Starting Point

In creating Foodies, I opted for qualitative research methods during
design process. I had the ability to ask specific questions about our
audience, issues that may arise and how I can resolve those issues.

Research Insights

I conducted a user questionnaire to have a better understanding what users
seek for in a meal delivery service. After interviewing potential users, I was
able to identify pain points and what features users would look for.

Meet the Users





Userflow

I constructed a userflow of what a basic start to finish journey
looks like when purchasing meals. This helps me in understanding
the way users could interact with the product and also helps
prevent future pain points for the users.

Takeaways

Being a food lover and at home chef myself, working on Foodies was rewarding.
Becoming an UX designer, there is a lot of room for me to grow and I chose to create
Foodies to challenge myself. I wanted to experiement with micro-interactions and
produce something I'm proud of as a beginner in the industry.
